Instructions

You are in a room with eight doors. There are monsters outside trying to break in and attack you. The aim of the game is to stay alive as long as possible.

Whenever a door changes colour, this means a monster is trying to break in and attack you. After the door changes colour, you have 4 seconds to protect that door, by pressing space bar, before the monster breaks in. When a monster does break in, it is game over.
